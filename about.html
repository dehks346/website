Our Philosophy

Each patient is approached as individual. There is no “one size fits all” formula, as no two people are the same. I discuss with my patients the treatment methods I intend to use and adapt or change my approach as needed to suit the individual in front of me. I encourage clear and open communication and strive to maintain a relaxed but professional environment where patients feel comfortable, understood, and in control. Asking questions is encouraged, as educating patients about their situation is important for proper understanding and engagement with the treatment process.
