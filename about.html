About Bumble and Flow

Bumble and Flow is a holistic wellness clinic offering an integrated approach to chiropractic care and reiki energy healing. Founded by Dr Rosie Hitchcock, a Doctor of Chiropractic, the clinic focuses on supporting the body as a whole rather than treating symptoms in isolation. By combining evidence-based chiropractic techniques with gentle energy work, Bumble and Flow helps restore balance, improve movement, and encourage the body’s natural healing processes. Every session is delivered with care and intention, creating a calm environment where clients can feel supported, grounded, and at ease.

Chiropractic care and reiki energy healing work together to support the nervous system, reduce stress, and restore balance throughout the body.

Whether you are seeking relief from pain, improved mobility, or a deeper sense of wellbeing, Bumble and Flow offers personalised sessions tailored to your individual needs. Dr Rosie Hitchcock takes a warm, client-centred approach, focusing on long-term health as well as immediate comfort. The aim is to help you move, feel, and live with greater ease, balance, and flow.
