What is Chiropractic Care

Chiropractic care is a healthcare approach focused on diagnosing and treating issues related to the spine, joints, and nervous system. Through precise, hands-on techniques, chiropractic treatment aims to restore proper alignment, reduce pain, improve mobility, and support the body’s natural ability to heal and function optimally.
