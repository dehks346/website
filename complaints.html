How a patient can raise a complaint (informally or formally)

Informally - feedback is always welcome during a session or an informal meeting can be arranged by request

Formally - a written complaint send via email

The timeframes for acknowledging and responding to the complaint

Informal complaints will be acknowledged within 3 working days

Formal complaints will be acknowledged within 5 working days

All complaints will be recieved with empathy and understanding aiming to resolve any issues or miscommunication as soon as possible.

What to do if the patient is still unhappy, including signposting to the GCC

If you are not satisfied then you have every right to contact the gcc via their "Make a Complaint Page" https://www.gcc-uk.org/concerns-about-a-chiropractor/make-a-complaint
