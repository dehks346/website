Rosie Hitchcock

Licensed Chiropractor & Reiki Master

Rosie is a bubbly, warm, and approachable individual who has always cared deeply about making the world a better place. Her interest in chiropractic care began at a young age through supporting her mum, who has received chiropractic treatment for many years to help manage the long-term effects of having polio as a child. Growing up, Rosie often helped her mum between treatments and naturally had a knack for finding areas of the body that were painful and helping them feel better.

After training at AECC University in Bournemouth, Rosie now supports her patients in the same instinctive way she once supported her mum, but with the added knowledge, clinical training, and experience to deliver safe, effective, and professional care.

It is recommended to wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows for easy movement during your session. This will help ensure you can fully benefit from your treatment.

No, a GP referral is not required. You can book an appointment directly with us.

Your first appointment will include an assessment of your individual needs and a discussion of the best approach for your care.
